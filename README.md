# ÍY SȻÁĆEL (Good Day/Hello) 👋🏽

<br>

My name is **Caleb M. Irvine** (He/Him). I am a **STÁUTW (Tsawout) First Nation** member, and a student in my second year, approaching my final Capstone Project summer term in the Information and Computer Systems Technologist Diploma program at Camosun College.

I am deeply passionate about the entire Apple ecosystem. Drawing from a strong foundation in Apple hardware repair and sales, I currently serve as a **Systems Analyst**, where I specialize in designing and implementing Apple-based workflow solutions, as well as building internal **Power Automate** flows and **Power Apps** to streamline organizational processes.

I follow Indigenous-led technology solutions, along with data sovereignty movements and I hope to contribute my skills to further these developments. I aim to support further Indigenous presence in technology, ensuring that as we advance, we align modern tools with cultural preservation.

I look forward to the new connections I meet in the community, and to strengthening the connections I continue to have.
